The idea of providing seamless property solutions to all Established in 2022, Kalpurush Real Estate specializes in residential and commercial properties across Durgapur, Kolkata, and West Bengal. We cater to both rental and premium registered properties, ensuring quality service for all our clients.

1. Residential Properties

Rental & Outright Sales: Flats, row houses, penthouses, villas, and residential plots ranging from 500 sq. ft. to acres.
Premium Properties: Exclusive flats priced between ₹20 lakh and ₹60 crore in posh areas developed by reputed builders.

2. Commercial Properties

Rental & Outright Sales: Offices, IT setups, shops, and showrooms.
Preleased Properties: Commercial offices, showrooms, and hotels with investment options from ₹2 crore to ₹50 crore, offering an ROI of 6%-9%.
